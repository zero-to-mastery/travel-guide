Welcome to Ireland

Congrats on making it to the Emerald Isle. There are a few places you should visit whilst you're here!

You should definitely visit the capital, Dublin, and make sure you get yourself a pint of the black stuff. Temple Bar has great pubs and take a stroll through St. Stephen's Green if it isn't raining (good luck)

You'll also want to make the most of the outdoors. Beautiful green landscape and picturesque beaches. My favourites are in County Donegal.
